Dancer and Performer for Babson Dance Ensemble

https://www.youtube.com/embed/FAfVEEGXXSc Spring 2011 - May 2014

Han probably dances more than he walks. Expressing his mood, emotions, and thoughts through the form of freestyle dancing is a skill he does not take for granted. So don't be surprised to find him working alone tapping his feet to music or swaying his head and shoulders left and right-- it's just him always being prepared to dance battle you at any given moment.

Being a member of the Babson Dance Ensemble allowed Han to perform on stage in front of hundreds of viewers under the big bright lights. It taught him how to be a showman, a performer, and a crowd pleaser.

He learned how to be himself on stage. As a man of confidence, his swagger is greatly attributed to his dancing capabilities.
